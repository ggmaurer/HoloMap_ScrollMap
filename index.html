My style is called Holo Map and is based on different holographic maps from Star Wars and other SciFi films.

I took inspiration from some of my favorite films and video games such as,

Star Wars: Jedi Fallen Order and Prometheus.

Cities within the map are labeled with the symbol of the Jedi from the Star Wars Trilogy.

The map "decays" as you zoom in.

Take a look at a few different geographies, starting with Seattle, Washington.

Making the buildings in downtown New York transparent helps them pop out like that of a hologram!

Rocky Mounatin National Park.

Cairo, Egypt and the Sahara Desert.
